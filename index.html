4th International Conference on

“Physics for Sustainable Development and Technology (ICPSDT-2022)”

22 – 23 January 2022

This site contains the list of poster presentations with videos and posters presented at the 4th International Conference on “Physics for Sustainable Development and Technology (ICPSDT-2022)” organized by Department of Physics, Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology, Chattogram 4349, Bangladesh. The two days long conference was held online from 22 January to 23 January in the year of 2022. The videos can also be watched from the YouTube Playlist. For the conference the site was created by Dr. Mohammad Abdur Rashid, a member of the organizing committee.
